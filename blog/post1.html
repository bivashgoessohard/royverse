5 Must-Have Gadgets Under ₹1000 in India

Published on September 01, 2025

In a world driven by technology, you don't have to break the bank to own some cool and useful gadgets. Here are our top 5 picks for must-have gadgets under ₹1000 in India.

1. A Reliable Power Bank

A power bank is a lifesaver for anyone who's always on the go. For under ₹1000, you can get a decent 10,000mAh power bank that can charge your smartphone at least twice. Look for brands like Mi, Ambrane, or Realme.

2. Bluetooth Earphones

Wireless is the way to go, and you can find some great Bluetooth earphones for under ₹1000. Brands like boAt, Mivi, and Noise offer impressive sound quality and battery life for the price.

3. A Smart LED Bulb

Transform your room with a smart LED bulb. You can control the color and brightness from your smartphone, and some even work with voice assistants like Alexa and Google Assistant. Wipro and Syska are popular choices in this category.

4. A Flexible Phone Tripod

For the aspiring photographer or vlogger, a flexible phone tripod is a must-have. These tripods can be wrapped around almost anything, allowing you to get the perfect shot from any angle. Look for options from brands like GorillaPod.

5. A USB-Powered Desk Fan

Stay cool while you work with a USB-powered desk fan. These compact fans are surprisingly powerful and can be plugged into your laptop or a power bank. They are perfect for a home office or a stuffy dorm room.
